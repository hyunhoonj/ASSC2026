Presidential Address — Focusing attention within and between perception and memory

Day 1 · Tue Jun 30🕑 16:30 – 17:30📍 Salon Fresno

발표자

Kia Nobre (Yale University)

Kia Nobre is a cognitive neuroscientist at Yale University, where she directs the Center for Neurocognition and Behavior at the Wu Tsai Institute. Her research examines how the brain dynamically and proactively prioritizes and selects information from the sensory stream and from memories to form psychological experience and guide behavior. She obtained her PhD at Yale (1993) and did postdoctoral research at Harvard Medical School. Before returning to Yale (2023) she spent ~30 years at the University of Oxford. She is President of the ASSC.
